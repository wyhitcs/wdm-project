The complexity of function can grow exponentially with dimension, there are more time needed in the training and testing procedure. If a similar accuracy in low dimension is need to estimate a function, we have to grow the size of training set exponentially in high dimension, therefore the calculation time needed is also exponential increase. It is important to narrow down the dimensionality, which does not only make the complexity high, but also lower the accuracy of prediction. The rise of dimensionality makes the coverage rate high as well. To capture a fraction of dataset, the rise of dimensionality also brings the needed of high coverage range in each coordination, which is known as the curse of dimensionality.
